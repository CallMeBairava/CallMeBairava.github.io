About The Designing Club

The Designing Club is a student-driven initiative under the Computer Science Students Association (CSSA), functioning within the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at CARE College of Engineering. The club serves as a dynamic platform for students to explore, learn, and apply skills in graphic design, web design, and UI/UX. It fosters a culture of creativity, innovation, and collaboration among its members.

What We Do

The Designing Club is committed to nurturing design talent through active learning and real-world experiences. It encourages students to transform ideas into compelling visual solutions while building a strong foundation in modern design tools and practices.

  • Hands-on workshops on graphic design, web design, and UI/UX fundamentals
  • Real-time design projects and collaboration with other clubs and departments
  • Design challenges and hackathons to promote creative problem-solving
  • Opportunities to build professional portfolios and gain practical exposure
  • Peer learning, mentorship, and knowledge-sharing sessions
  • Focus on preparing students for design roles in the tech industry
